Thank you for volunteering for the Penn-West Philadelphia Community Partnership Celebration Day on Saturday, October 9th!

A few final instructions:

If you plan on going to the Sayre School from campus via the Penn Bus:
The buses will leave on Saturday between 8:30 am and 3:00 pm, departures roughly every 30 minutes, from two locations:

-In front of Houston Hall (3417 Spruce Street)
-39th and Walnut Streets

The bus will drop off passengers directly in front of the Sayre School, where the Registration Tables will be.

Buses will be returning to campus from the Sayre School throughout the day

If you plan on going to the Sayre School on your own:

The Sayre School is located on Walnut Street, between 58th and 59th Streets. Parking is available in the parking lot behind the school, via 59th Street. Once you have parked, please walk around to the front of the school (along Walnut Street) to the Registration tables.

Please remember:

-We will be engaged in painting and cleaning projects. Please wear comfortable work clothes that you will not mind getting dirty
-Breakfast and lunch will be provided for volunteers
-If you have any questions before Saturday, call Civic House at 215-898-4831
